The Flame That Cooked Our Roots

From a rainy balcony to the buzz of P138 Street, Flexy Chicken was born from fire, flavor, and fierce ambition.

Sinethemba Zondo started Flexy Chicken in 2018 with just a small grill and a big dream. What began as a way to feed her family quickly became the talk of Inanda, with neighbors lining up for her signature flame-grilled chicken.

Today, Flexy Chicken stands as a testament to township entrepreneurship, employing 15 local staff and serving over 500 meals daily. Our mission goes beyond food — we're building community, one flame-grilled piece at a time.
